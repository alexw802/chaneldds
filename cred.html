20+ Years of Experience

Dr. Chanel Ko Wiederkehr, DDS began practicing dentistry in 1999 after graduating from USC Dental School after departing careers in art and modeling.
She loves making and improving smiles, and the satisfaction of helping her patients.


In 1996, Chanel published her award winning essay "Future Dentistry in Ethics" in The Journal of the American College of Dentists.
In 1998, she became a Presidential Fellow of the Marshall School of Business.
